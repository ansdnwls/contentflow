2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use ContentFlow:

2-1. TikTok Account Information

When you connect your TikTok account to ContentFlow, we access basic account information provided by TikTok through its official API, including:

  • TikTok user ID and display name

We do not collect or store your TikTok login credentials (username or password). Authentication is handled entirely through TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 process.

2-2. Content Data

ContentFlow processes content you create or generate through the App, including video files, scripts, metadata (titles, descriptions, tags), and publishing schedules. This data is used solely to fulfill your content publishing requests.

2-3. Usage Data

We may collect non-personal usage information such as the frequency of App usage, features accessed, and error logs. This information is used to improve App performance and user experience.
